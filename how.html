What is StudioBid?

For active students

StudioBid is a concept auction platform for creative students. List tools, gear, art and supplies you no longer use and bid on the pieces that fit your next project.

Safe & simple

Only @stud.noroff.no accounts can register and bid, so you're trading within a focused student community. Clear rules, credits and transparent bidding keep things straightforward.

For bidders

1. Create your account

Sign up with your @stud.noroff.no email to unlock bidding, profile editing and listing creation.

2. Get your starting credits

Your account shows how many credits you have available. Use them as a guide when deciding how high you want to bid.

3. Browse listings

Use search and filters on the front page to find tablets, cameras, paints, prints and more from other students.

4. Place bids & follow your auctions

Open a listing, place a bid higher than the current one and confirm. Track everything under My bids on your profile.

For sellers

1. Create a listing

Click Create listing in the header to start a new auction for your item.

2. Add details and images

Give it a clear title, short description and image URLs that show the item from its best angles.

3. Publish and share

Set an end date and time, then publish. Your listing appears on the front page and can be discovered through search.

4. Auction ends automatically

When the end time is reached, no more bids can be placed. You can review the final bid history in your listing details.

Without an account

  • Browse all active auctions.
  • Search listings by keyword.
  • Open listings to see images, descriptions and bid history.

With an account

  • Create and manage your own listings.
  • Place bids on other users’ auctions.
  • See your credits in the header on every page.
  • Edit your bio, avatar and banner on your profile.
  • Track activity in My listings, My bids and My wins.

Credits

Credits represent how much you can reasonably bid across auctions. When you’re logged in, your current credits are always visible in the header so you can keep track while you browse and bid.

Bidding rules

  • You must be logged in with a @stud.noroff.no email to place bids.
  • You can’t bid on your own listings.
  • Your bid must always be higher than the current highest bid.
  • When the end date and time is reached, the auction closes and no new bids can be placed.

Community & guidelines

  • Use clear, respectful language in titles and descriptions.
  • Don’t upload or link to illegal, hateful or harmful content.
  • Only use images and links you have the right to share.
  • Remember there are real people behind each listing and bid – treat other creatives with kindness.
